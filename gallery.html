George Russell

Russell has grown into a leadership role within Mercedes, balancing speed with a deep understanding of race strategy.

He is known for his precision in wheel-to-wheel racing and his ability to stay composed under pressure. His performances are

consistently strong, even when the car is not the fastest on the grid. As a driver entering his prime, he remains a constant threat

for race wins and titles.

Kimi Antonelli

Antonelli has taken Formula 1 by storm, quickly establishing himself as one of the most exciting young talents in years. His aggressive yet controlled driving style allows him to overtake decisively without excessive risk. Even as a teenager, he demonstrates remarkable confidence and adaptability across different circuits. Mercedes sees him as the cornerstone of their future, with expectations of multiple championships.

Lewis Hamilton

Hamilton’s legacy as one of the greatest drivers in Formula 1 history is firmly established. His experience gives him a unique advantage in reading races and adapting strategies on the fly. Even in the later stages of his career, he remains highly competitive and capable of winning races. At Ferrari, he plays a dual role as both a contender and a mentor figure within the team.

Charles Leclerc

Leclerc remains one of the fastest drivers over a single lap, often delivering spectacular pole positions. His aggressive driving style can produce brilliant results, though he has worked on improving consistency during races. He carries the hopes of Ferrari fans as he continues to pursue his first world championship. When everything comes together, he is nearly unstoppable.

Lando Norris

Norris has fully matured into a world championship-winning driver, combining natural speed with exceptional consistency across a full season. He is especially strong in qualifying, often extracting the maximum from the car in high-pressure moments. Over time, he has refined his racecraft, showing patience in battles and smarter tyre management. As McLaren’s leader, he sets the standard and carries the responsibility of defending his title against a hungry field.

Oscar Piastri

Piastri continues to impress with his calm, calculated driving style that rarely cracks under pressure. His technical feedback is highly valued by engineers, helping McLaren stay competitive at the front. Despite being early in his career, he has already shown he can win races and challenge top drivers consistently. Many see him as a future world champion, especially given how closely he matches Norris.

Esteban Ocon

Ocon is known for his determination and resilience, often holding his ground in tough battles. He is a consistent points scorer when given a competitive car. His defensive driving can be particularly difficult for rivals to overcome. With experience on his side, he acts as a steady presence within Haas.
